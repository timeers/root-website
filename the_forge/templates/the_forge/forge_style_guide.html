The Forge

Forge Style Guide

A reference for the elements, fonts, and conventions used to build a faction board.

Typography

Root uses two typefaces. Decorative titles are set in Luminari; body text is set in Baskerville 10 Pro (with Bold, Italic, and Bold-Italic variants).

Luminari

Decorative titles and headings.

  • Section Titles
  • Boxes (Satchel & Officers)
  • Cardboard Track Headers (Prowess & Command)
  • Card Outlines (Mood & Leader Cards)

Baskerville 10 Pro

Body text.

  • Bold for emphasis
  • Italics for reminders and thematic text
  • Large for Phase Step names and Actions
