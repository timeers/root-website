Welcome to the {{site_title}}, an unofficial collection of fan factions and components for Root, the board game by Leder Games. The goal of the site is to provide an easy place to search for Fan Content made by the Root community with links to discussion boards and Discord threads. You can post game results to track your stats and search community resources to help make your own creations. If you play a game with any of the components found here you can record that game to help the creators see their faction's stats.
If you have any questions about how the site works, check out the FAQ.
Feedback, bug reports, and feature requests can be submitted here. You can also ask questions and give feedback in the Root Database Discord.
If you haven't already, be sure to Join the Woodland Warriors Discord, the official home of Root and all Leder Games boardgames. It's a great place to discuss strategy and find online games.

Root Digital League
Root Digital League is organized by the Woodland Warriors Discord community. League games are played through the Direwolf Digital app and are structured to be an informal and fun way to play against real players. These games are recorded on the Root Digital League website and then imported to the {{site_title}} through the Match Viewset List API provided by Pliskin.
