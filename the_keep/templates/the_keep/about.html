Welcome to the {{site_title}}, an unofficial collection of components for Root, the board game by Leder Games. The goal of the site is to provide an easy place to search for Fan Content made by the Root community with links to discussion boards and Discord threads. You can post game results to track your stats and search community resources to help make your own creations.
If you have any questions about how the site works, check out the FAQ.
Feedback, bug reports, and feature requests can be submitted here. You can also find a more detailed FAQ, ask questions, and give feedback on the RDB Feedback Discord.
If you haven't already, be sure to Join the Woodland Warriors Discord, the official home of Root and all Leder Games boardgames. It's a great place to discuss strategy and find online games.
