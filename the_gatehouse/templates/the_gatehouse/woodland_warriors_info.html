Woodland Warriors

Woodland Warriors is the official Discord community for Root and Leder Games. It's where players organize games, discuss strategy, compete in tournaments, and share fan content.

The Root Database is not officially affiliated with the Woodland Warriors community, but membership allows us to reach out to users when needed.

Joining gives you access to:
Record and track your games
Participate in and create tournaments, leagues, and game groups
Submit your fan content
Build your Faction from scratch in the Forge
Create and take community surveys
How to get access
  1. Join the Woodland Warriors Discord server using the button below.
  2. Log out of Root Database and log back in via Discord.
  3. Your account will automatically be updated.
